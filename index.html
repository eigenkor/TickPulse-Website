Everything you need to trade by ear

Six audio modules working together give you a complete picture of market microstructure — without looking at a screen.

Spatial Stereo Separation

Buys in your left ear, sells in your right. Feel the pressure shift between buyers and sellers in real-time without reading a single number.

Dominance Ratio Engine

The DR tracks buy vs sell pressure on a rolling tick window. When it crosses 0.50, you hear the momentum flip — seconds before it shows on a chart.

Block Trade Gong

Institutional-size orders trigger a deep metallic gong. The bigger the trade, the louder it rings. Rapid gongs mean someone large is in the market.

Fake Spike Detector

Detects abnormal velocity spikes that are likely stop-runs. A warning alarm fires, then a reversal sweep confirms the fake — so you take the opposite side.

Exhaustion Alerts

When BPM peaks then rapidly drops, momentum is dying. You hear a descending sweep 2-5 seconds before the move fades on your chart.

13 Sound Presets

Click, bell, chime, pop, blip, tone, alarm, sweep, siren, gong, drum, buzz, glass — each a distinct synthesis recipe you can assign to any module.
